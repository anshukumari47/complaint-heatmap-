About CivicPulse

Bridging Citizens & Government

A platform designed to make urban governance transparent and responsive.

🎯 The Problem

Citizens face a fragmented and opaque system when reporting civic issues. Complaints get lost, there's no accountability, and residents have no way to track if their concerns are being addressed.

💡 Our Solution

CivicPulse creates a transparent pipeline: citizens submit geo-tagged complaints, authorities see density heatmaps to prioritize, and everyone can track resolution status in real time.

Our Objectives

1

Enable citizens to easily report infrastructure problems with precise location data

2

Provide authorities with visual heatmaps to identify high-priority problem zones

3

Create accountability through transparent public tracking of complaint resolution

4

Reduce duplicate complaints and wasted effort with intelligent deduplication
